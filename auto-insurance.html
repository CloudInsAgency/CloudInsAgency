Auto Insurance

Comprehensive vehicle coverage that keeps you protected on the road

Auto insurance is essential protection for you, your passengers, and your vehicle. Whether you're commuting to work, running errands, or taking a road trip, the right auto insurance gives you peace of mind. As an independent agency, we compare quotes from multiple top-rated carriers to find you the best coverage at competitive rates.

Types of Auto Insurance Coverage

Liability Coverage

Required in New Jersey to cover damages to others.

  • Bodily injury liability
  • Property damage liability
  • Legal defense costs
  • State minimum requirements
  • Higher limits available

Collision Coverage

Protects your vehicle from accident damage.

  • Single-car accidents
  • Multi-vehicle collisions
  • Hit-and-run incidents
  • Rollover accidents
  • Various deductible options

Comprehensive Coverage

Coverage for non-collision incidents.

  • Theft and vandalism
  • Weather damage (hail, flood)
  • Fire damage
  • Falling objects
  • Animal collisions

Personal Injury Protection

Required coverage in New Jersey.

  • Medical expenses
  • Lost wages coverage
  • Essential services
  • Death benefits
  • No-fault coverage

Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist

Protection when others lack adequate coverage.

  • Bodily injury coverage
  • Property damage coverage
  • Hit-and-run protection
  • Medical expense coverage
  • Lost wages protection

Additional Coverages

Optional enhancements for extra protection.

  • Roadside assistance
  • Rental car reimbursement
  • Gap insurance
  • Custom parts & equipment
  • Towing coverage

Understanding Your Auto Insurance Coverage

Bodily Injury Liability

Covers medical expenses, lost wages, and legal fees if you're at fault in an accident that injures others.

Property Damage

Pays for damage to another person's property (vehicle, fence, building) if you're found at fault in an accident.

Medical Payments

Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers, regardless of who's at fault in an accident.

Rental Reimbursement

Pays for a rental car while your vehicle is being repaired after a covered accident or theft.
