What Building Coverage Does NOT Cover

Understanding exclusions prevents surprises:

NOT Covered by Building Coverage:

  • Personal Property/Belongings - Requires separate Contents Coverage
  • Landscaping - Trees, shrubs, plants, fences, decks, patios
  • Swimming Pools - The pool structure itself (equipment IS covered)
  • Detached Structures Over 10% - Sheds, detached garages have limited coverage
  • Basement Living Space Improvements - Finished basement flooring, walls, ceilings (limited coverage)
  • Currency, Precious Metals, Valuables - These go under Contents Coverage
  • Additional Living Expenses - NFIP doesn't cover temporary housing (some private policies do)

How to File a Flood Building Coverage Claim in Essex County

If flooding occurs, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure Safety First: Don't enter a flooded building until safe to do so
  2. Contact Cloud Insurance Immediately: (973) 449-3671 - We'll help you start the claim
  3. Document Everything: Take photos/videos of all damage before cleanup
  4. Call Your Flood Insurer: Report the claim within 60 days (required)
  5. Mitigate Further Damage: Take reasonable steps to prevent additional damage
  6. Keep All Receipts: Save receipts for emergency repairs and cleanup
  7. Don't Throw Out Damaged Items: Adjuster needs to see everything
  8. Complete Proof of Loss Form: Required within 60 days of flood
  9. Work with Adjuster: They'll inspect and assess damage
  10. Get Multiple Contractor Bids: For repair work

‚è∞ Critical Deadlines for Essex County Flood Claims

60 Days to Report Claim - Don't wait! Call immediately after flooding.

60 Days to Submit Proof of Loss - Detailed form of damages and costs.

Missing these deadlines can result in claim denial. Cloud Insurance helps you meet all requirements.
