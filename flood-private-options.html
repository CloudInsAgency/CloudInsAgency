Private Flood Insurance Myths Debunked

Myth #1: "Private flood insurance is more expensive"

Reality: Private insurance is often LESS expensive than NFIP, especially for newer, well-maintained, or elevated homes. We regularly see savings of 20-40%.

Myth #2: "My lender won't accept private flood insurance"

Reality: Federal law requires lenders to accept private flood insurance that meets standards. It's been this way since 2019.

Myth #3: "Private insurance won't cover high-risk areas"

Reality: Private insurers do cover high-risk areas. While rates may be higher for extremely high-risk properties, many Essex County "high-risk" homes still get competitive private rates.

Myth #4: "NFIP is more secure because it's government-backed"

Reality: Private insurers are financially strong, regulated companies. They pay claims just as reliably‚Äîoften faster‚Äîthan NFIP.

Myth #5: "I'm required to have NFIP"

Reality: If you're in a high-risk zone with a federally backed mortgage, you're required to have flood insurance‚Äîbut it doesn't have to be NFIP. Private insurance satisfies the requirement.
