Why You Need Umbrella Insurance

An umbrella policy provides an extra layer of liability protection that goes beyond the limits of your home and auto insurance. It's surprisingly affordable and can protect your assets from major claims.

Protect Your Assets

Shields your savings, investments, home equity, and future earnings from lawsuits that exceed your standard policy limits.

Broader Coverage

Covers liability situations that may not be covered by standard policies, including false arrest, libel, and slander.

Worldwide Protection

Most umbrella policies provide coverage anywhere in the world, protecting you even when traveling internationally.

Affordable Premiums

Umbrella coverage is surprisingly affordable, often costing just a few hundred dollars annually for $1 million in coverage.

Life Insurance Options

Term Life Insurance

Affordable coverage for a specific period of time, perfect for covering mortgages, income replacement, or protecting your family during working years.

  • 10, 20, or 30-year terms
  • Level premium payments
  • Renewable options
  • Convertible to permanent

Whole Life Insurance

Permanent coverage that builds cash value over time, providing lifelong protection and a savings component for your family.

  • Lifetime coverage guarantee
  • Cash value accumulation
  • Fixed premium payments
  • Dividend potential

Universal Life Insurance

Flexible permanent coverage that allows you to adjust premiums and death benefits as your needs change throughout life.

  • Flexible premium payments
  • Adjustable death benefit
  • Cash value growth
  • Investment options

Protecting Your Valuables

Standard home insurance policies have limits on high-value items like jewelry, art, and collectibles. Scheduled personal property coverage ensures these treasured possessions are fully protected.

Agreed Value Coverage

We agree on the value of your items upfront, so there's no dispute about replacement cost in the event of a loss.

No Deductible

Most valuable items policies don't have a deductible, meaning you get the full replacement value if something happens.

Broad Coverage

Protection against accidental loss, mysterious disappearance, and damage - not just theft or fire like standard policies.

Worldwide Protection

Your valuables are covered anywhere in the world, whether at home or traveling.
