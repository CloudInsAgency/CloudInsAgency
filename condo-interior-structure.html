Understanding Master Policy Types in Detail

1. "Bare Walls-In" (Studs-In) Master Policy:

This is the most common type in Essex County condos. The HOA covers the building structure, but you're responsible for everything inside your walls.

  • HOA Covers: Building frame, exterior walls, roof, common areas
  • You Cover: Drywall, paint, flooring, cabinets, countertops, fixtures, appliances, doors, trim
  • HO-6 Coverage Needed: Most comprehensive ($40K-$150K+ typical)

2. "All-In" (Single Entity) Master Policy:

Less common, but some newer Essex County condos have this coverage. The HOA covers the building AND all original fixtures and finishes.

  • HOA Covers: Building structure, all original installations (cabinets, fixtures, flooring, appliances)
  • You Cover: Only improvements/upgrades beyond original construction
  • HO-6 Coverage Needed: Lower ($10K-$50K for improvements only)

3. "Bare Walls-Out" Master Policy:

Rare, but the most limited HOA coverage. You're responsible for even more than "Bare Walls-In."

  • HOA Covers: Only exterior structural walls outward
  • You Cover: Interior structural walls, everything inside
  • HO-6 Coverage Needed: Highest ($50K-$200K+)
